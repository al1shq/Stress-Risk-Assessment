If you are experiencing severe stress, anxiety, or other mental health concerns, please seek support from a licensed mental health professional. For immediate help, consider reaching out to a trusted counselor, therapist, or a mental health hotline in your area.

Rate each statement based on how often it applies to you, from 1 (Never) to 5 (Very Often). Answer honestly for the best results.

1. Do you feel pressured to meet expectations set by friends, family, or society?

2. How frequently do you compare yourself to your peers in terms of achievements or social status?

3. How often do you struggle to find time to relax or engage in activities you enjoy?

4. Do you feel disconnected or isolated from your friends or family?

5. How often are you excited to try new experiences?

6. Have personal responsibilities (such as caring for siblings, household chores, or part-time jobs) negatively impacted your mental well-being?

7. How often do you suppress personal struggles or emotions to avoid burdening others?

8. Have changes in family dynamics (such as divorce, financial instability, or moving) significantly affected your mental well-being?

9. How often are you worried about conflicts at home?

10. Do you feel supported by your family when dealing with stress?

11. How frequently do you feel that your workload is unmanageable?

12. How often do you experience stress due to upcoming deadlines, exams, or grades?

13. Do you feel anxious about future academic or career prospects?

14. How frequently do you sacrifice sleep to complete school-related tasks?

15. Do you believe your stress improves your academic performance?

16. How often do you experience fatigue, headaches, or other physical symptoms?

17. Do you find it difficult to maintain healthy eating, exercise, or sleep habits?

18. How frequently do you procrastinate or struggle with motivation due to stress?

19. How often do you feel overwhelmed by responsibilities from multiple areas of your life (school, home, extracurriculars, social life)?

20. Do you feel in control of your time and daily schedule?
